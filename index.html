ABOUT THE BOOK

Every Great Adventure Begins With One Feather...

Discover a Magical World

Deep inside the Whispering Forest, Jocky discovers a mysterious glowing golden feather. Little does he know... it belongs to Aurora, the legendary Golden Bird who protects the happiness of the forest.

Together with his friends, Jocky follows magical clues, solves ancient riddles, crosses broken bridges, and uncovers secrets hidden deep within enchanted caves.
